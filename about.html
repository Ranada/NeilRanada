ABOUT ME

Neil Ranada

|

UX Researcher & Designer

Los Angeles, CA

Hi, I’m Neil. I’m a User Experience Researcher and Designer based out of Los Angeles, CA.

I have 10 years of professional experience with a background in Aviation, Aerospace, and Defense. I was a Navy Helicopter Pilot while holding various managerial roles. I’ve been solving problems in small teams throughout my career.

I’m interested in making mobile apps, websites, and other digital products easier to use for people. Technology should make our lives easier not harder. A well-intentioned but poorly designed product can lead to a person experiencing frustration.

What’s the cost of frustration? Frustration can result in customers being discouraged from buying your products or using your services. It can also distract your workers from doing productive work. That’s time, resources, and money $$$ I’d like to help you save!

In my free time, I like to surf, hike, and travel.

Contact me if you want to work together. Or, if you just want to say “hi” and chat, that’s cool too!
